About Dboi

With 15 years in the tattoo industry, I've been honored to create art that clients carry forever. Starting in a small studio, I worked my way through conventions across the USA, building relationships with clients and artists nationwide.

This journey led me to open StayOnPoint Tattoo Studio in NYC, where I specialize in photorealistic portraits and large-scale black and grey work. Our studio has become a platform for traveling artists worldwide, showcasing talent in one of the most vibrant cities on earth.

We've accomplished so much over the years, and I'm truly blessed to continue being a platform for aspiring artists while serving clients who trust me with their most meaningful tattoo visions. The relationships built through this art form—with clients, fellow artists, and the global tattoo community—remain the most rewarding part of this journey.
